Common Questions

Q. How much do deliveries cost?

We mainly deliver in Dhaka city. Inside Dhaka city it is 80 BDT and outside Dhaka it is 120 BDT.

Q. What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 8.00 am to 11 pm every day. You can choose from available slots to find something that is convenient for you.

Q: What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of purchase, provided the item is unused and in its original packaging. Please contact customer support to initiate a return.

Q: Can I cancel or change my order?

Orders can be canceled or modified within the first 24 hours. Please contact customer support immediately.
